Register

›› Create your account by clicking on sign-up.
›› On the dropdown choose the account type.
›› A box will appear where you will be prompted to enter information such
as your name, email address and password.

Buy / Bid

›› Preview the product details.
›› Double check minimum ammout allowed to bid.

to spend.
